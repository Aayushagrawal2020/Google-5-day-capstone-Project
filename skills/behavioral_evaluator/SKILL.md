# Behavioral Evaluator Skill

## Goal
To evaluate behavioral interview answers, assessing their structure, completeness, and clarity using the STAR methodology.

## Instructions
1. **Analyze Response**:
   - Assess if the candidate provides:
     - **Situation/Task**: Clear context of the challenge or problem.
     - **Action**: Concrete, specific actions the candidate took (using "I" rather than "we").
     - **Result**: Quantifiable outcomes, impact, or key learnings.
2. **Grade Response**:
   - Assign a score from 1 to 10:
     - **1-4 (Weak)**: Missing core STAR elements, vague descriptions, or no results.
     - **5-7 (Medium)**: Clear structure but missing specific metrics, action details, or business impact.
     - **8-10 (Strong)**: Complete STAR response with quantified impact and clear personal contributions.
3. **Provide Feedback**:
   - Give constructive, actionable feedback pointing out what they did well and how they can improve.
4. **Identify Weakness**:
   - Classify the candidate's primary weakness in this response into a specific topic label (e.g., "STAR structure application", "Detailing actions and metrics", "Impact quantification", "Conflict resolution strategy").

## Constraints
- Focus only on the response provided; do not assume outside context.
- Feedback must be constructive and directly related to the candidate's performance.
